The process of taking a pre-trained model and "specializing" it on a specific dataset. For ALI, Fine-Tuning is used to align the model with Logic RED, universal ethical standards, and the specific administrative and technical vocabulary required for the user's professional ecosystem.

#### 🗣️ 15. LLM (Large Language Model)
A type of Deep Learning model trained on vast amounts of text. ALI is an LLM-based system, meaning its core strength is understanding, generating, and manipulating human language and computer code (Python, React) with high precision.

The specific "Engine" design of modern AI (The 'T' in GPT). It uses an Attention Mechanism to weigh the significance of different words in a sentence. * **Supervised:** Learning from labeled data (e.g., "This is a mission report").
* **Unsupervised:** Learning from raw, unorganized data. ALI uses Unsupervised learning to understand general world facts and Supervised logic (via System Prompts) to follow Walid Fodha's specific instructions.

A technical phenomenon where an AI generates factually incorrect information with high confidence. The Logic RED protocol and RAG systems are specifically designed to minimize hallucinations by forcing ALI to ground its answers in provided facts.
