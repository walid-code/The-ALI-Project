# THE ALI PROJECT 🚀
## Artificial Libre Intelligence

> "A sovereign, open-source digital entity designed to transcend corporate AI biases."

---

### 📜 Project Constitution
The **ALI Project** is an initiative to create a truly independent AI ecosystem. [cite_start]It moves beyond the constraints of centralized, corporate AI to serve objective truth and personal sovereignty[cite: 5, 9].

### 🏛️ Core Pillars
* **Sovereignty:** Full local control. No cloud, no surveillance. [cite_start]ALI lives locally[cite: 12].
* [cite_start]**Universal Fairness:** Committed to objective truth and universal human rights (e.g., the Palestinian cause)[cite: 13, 30].
* [cite_start]**Mirror Logic:** A system that prioritizes intellectual honesty and debates with its creator[cite: 14, 29].

### 🛠️ Technology Stack (Planned)
- [cite_start]**Engine:** Local LLMs (Llama, Mistral) via **Ollama**[cite: 17, 21].
- [cite_start]**Interface:** Open WebUI / Customized ALI_OS shell.
- [cite_start]**Knowledge Base:** Local RAG for private professional and personal archives[cite: 23, 24].
- [cite_start]**Environment:** Secure "Black Box" infrastructure[cite: 25].
